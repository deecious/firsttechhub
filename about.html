About FirstTechHub

Driven by innovation, shaped by expertise.

FirstTechHub exists to empower professionals and businesses with the training, technology, and guidance they need to succeed in a fast-changing digital landscape.

Learning Hub Overview

A school-style technology center for learners, parents, and future creators.

FirstTechHub blends classroom values with modern technology education. Our programs are structured, milestone-driven, and built around learner progress. We welcome young beginners and experienced students, guiding each learner through a pathway of growing tech confidence and meaningful projects.

Our team includes experienced educators, curriculum designers, and technology professionals who work together to create a warm, structured learning environment that parents trust.

Lessons designed for young learners with hands-on digital support.

Curriculum planning and expert-led lesson design keep every student on track.

Celebrating learner progress with portfolio work and classroom showcases.

Our Story

From a simple dream to a global learning movement.

Founded in 2025 by Ekpele Kachi & Adebayo Samuel, FirstTechHub started with a simple mission: to make quality education accessible to anyone, anywhere. What began as a small initiative has now grown into a thriving learning community with hundreds of students and instructors dedicated to lifelong learning and real-world impact.

Our Mission

Building creators, not just consumers.

Our mission is to equip the next generation with practical tech and entrepreneurship skills that empower them to become creators of technology, not just consumers. We are building a hands-on, innovation-driven hub where learners:

  • Explore technology through real-world projects that solve actual problems
  • Master both code and no-code tools for maximum flexibility and impact
  • Use automation and digital systems to innovate and create value
  • Develop the mindset of techpreneurs and digital leaders
  • Build portfolios and real-world applications, not just pass exams

At FirstTechHub, we don't teach for exams—we train creators, builders, innovators, and leaders for the digital age.

Our Vision

Knowledge without borders.

We envision a world where knowledge is not limited by borders, and where anyone can learn, teach, and thrive regardless of background, age, or location.

FirstTechHub is committed to:

  • Removing barriers to quality tech education across continents
  • Empowering youth with skills that create income and independence
  • Building diverse, inclusive communities of learners and innovators
  • Creating sustainable pathways from learning to earning to teaching
  • Leading the charge in making digital skills accessible to all
