Admission Policy

Assessment-based placement for every learner.

Our entry policy is designed to place learners in the right pathway, support experienced students, and give beginners a structured foundation.

Placement and Assessment

How we place learners in the right FirstTech pathway.

Assessment for experienced learners

Experienced learners take a skills review that ensures they enter a pathway aligned to their current knowledge. This prevents repetition and helps learners progress faster.

  • Skills review across digital tools, coding, and project experience
  • Placement guidance for Tech Builders, Tech Creators, or VIP Custom Pro
  • Personalized learning map after assessment

70% cut-off mark

For experienced applicants, we use a 70% assessment threshold to confirm readiness for advanced pathways. This standard protects the integrity of the program and ensures learners are placed appropriately.

  • Scores of 70% and above qualify for higher placement
  • Scores below 70% are advised to begin in a foundational pathway
  • All learners receive constructive feedback and a clear next step

Placement System

Intelligent placement, not guesswork.

How often do we assess?

Assessments are completed at enrollment and at major pathway transitions to confirm the next right step.

What happens after a 70% score?

Experienced learners are placed into a higher pathway or VIP Custom Pro if they demonstrate advanced readiness.

Do beginners need to take an assessment?

Yes — beginners take a gentle placement check so we can match them with the right start point and pace.

Can learners move pathways later?

Yes — progress reviews and assessments allow learners to move forward or change direction as needed.
